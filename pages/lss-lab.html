Large scale structures lab

Fully immerse yourself in the Large Scale Structures Lab. Play the video and drag the screen in any direction to view the lab from 360 degrees.
Located in the South Engineering Research Center, the Large Scale Structures Lab (LSSL) is equipped to test high force seismic and wind response. The 12-ft x 13-ft earthquake simulation shake table can withstand 20 tons and 1.2g acceleration. The lab's floor contains reaction blocks that can be arranged in various configurations for diversified testing.
