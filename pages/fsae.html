Students on Crimson Racing design and build a formula style racecar from scratch annually to compete in FSAE Michigan held at Michigan International Speedway. There are approximately 120 University teams from around the world that we compete against at Michigan each year in Static Events like Design and Business as well as Dynamic Events like Acceleration and Endurance Races. Crimson Racing is a fantastic program for building young engineers that will face tomorrow’s challenges, if you’re a student interested in joining or someone thinking about sponsoring our team, visit us online by searching Crimson Racing UA.
