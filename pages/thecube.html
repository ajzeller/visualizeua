The lab provides resources for students, staff, and faculty to design and create 3D printed objects. On-site staff can assist with concept drawings and file printing. The lab is equipped with multiple commercial grade 3D printers including the Stratasys Dimension SST 1200es, the Objet30 Pro, and 3D Systems Projet 460 Plus. Visit the Cube's webpage to submit a design to be printed. ---
