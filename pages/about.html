Our Mission

We started out as a mechanical engineering senior design team working to make an augmented and virtual reality tour of the University of Alabama's campus. We are developing an AR app to be used in the engineering college buildings. This website houses the VR content including 360 videos. Explore our website to learn more about points of interest on campus, view fully immersed content of these sites, and find out more about joining our team.

Contribute to our effort

We are actively seeking to recruit members to our organization. All majors from graphic design, TCF, computer science, and engineering are needed in this multi-disciplinary project. If you have an interest in learning about new augmented reality technology and its capabilities, we'll find a way for you to contribute. ​ If you are interested in joining our team or learning more about our project, please contact us at visualizeua@gmail.com.
